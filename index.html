About E-HORYZON'23

E-Horizon is a popular event at our college that conducts technical events every year. These events are designed to help students improve
their technical skills and knowledge, as well as provide an opportunity to showcase their talent and creativity. Student participation in technical
events is of utmost importance as it offers several benefits to their academic and personal growth. Firstly, it provides opportunity for students
to showcase their skills and knowledge in a particular field and gain recognition for their work. This can boost their confidence and encourage
them to pursue their interests further. Secondly, participation in technical events allows students to network with like-minded individuals, industry
professionals, and potential employers. This can lead to future job or internship opportunities and valuable connections in their chosen field.
